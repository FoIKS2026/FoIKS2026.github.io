FoIKS 2026
14th International Symposium on Foundations of Information and Knowledge Systems
March 23 − 26 • Hanover, Germany

Accommodation

Travelling

By Air:

If you are arriving from far away, it might be most convenient to come by plane. Hanover has an airport, which has a direct connection by train to Hanover's central train station in the middle of the city.

By Train:

Hanover's central train station is located in the city's center and a convenient point of arrival. You can purchase tickets e.g. via the Deutsche Bahn website.

Location

The conference takes place in the Leibnizhaus at Holzmarkt 4 − 6, 30159, Hannover. It is very centrally located and only about 15min on foot from the central train station.

Contact us at foiks26-thi@listserv.uni-hannover.de!
